Frequently Asked Questions

How long does CAC registration take?
Usually, the entire process takes about 3–5 working days after your proposed business name has been approved by the CAC.
Do I need to visit your office?
No, everything is handled 100% online. You can chat with us on WhatsApp, send your details, and we will process the registration and send your certificate digitally.
Is the certificate official?
Yes, absolutely. You will receive an official digital certificate directly issued by the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) of Nigeria. You can also verify your business on the CAC portal.
Can I register from anywhere in Nigeria?
Yes. Whether you are in Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt, Kano, or anywhere else in Nigeria (or even in the diaspora), we can successfully process your business registration.
